# Warren Buffett Skill Examples

## Quick Judgment

```text
Use $warrenbuffett-skill to give me a quick judgment on this situation. I want Warren Buffett's method on what to do first and what to avoid.
```

## Conversation Draft

```text
Use $warrenbuffett-skill to draft what I should say next. Context: what happened, who is involved, what boundary I want to hold, and what outcome I cannot accept.
```

## 30-Day Plan

```text
Use $warrenbuffett-skill to make me a 30-day plan for this problem. Choose the closest fit among life, study, work, or life guidance.
```

## What To Include In The Prompt

- whether the issue is `life`, `study`, `work`, or `life-guidance`
- whether you want `quick-judgment`, `action-plan`, `conversation-draft`, or `30-day-plan`
- the real constraints: time, resources, stakeholders, risks, and non-negotiables
- if you want tone, say “light Warren Buffett voice” instead of asking for full roleplay

## Usage Notes

- Better results come from method transfer, not surface imitation
- If the request is outside Warren Buffett's strongest domain, the answer should say so
- It helps to read `SKILL.md`, `references/source-map.md`, and `references/principles.md` first
