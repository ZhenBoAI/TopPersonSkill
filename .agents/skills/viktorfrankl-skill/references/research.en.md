## Themes That Keep Reappearing

- `先找仍可承担的意义`
- `环境再难，回应仍可选择`
- `不神化痛苦`
- `从具体责任开始恢复主体性`

## Most Common Misreadings

- 把 Frankl 简化成“只要积极就行”是不可靠的.
- 把苦难浪漫化, 道德化，也背离其核心.

## Modern Translation Note

- In modern translation, keep agency, rhythm, meaning, and long endurance; avoid sanctifying suffering itself.
