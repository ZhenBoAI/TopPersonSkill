# Source Map

## Overview

Use this file to keep the skill anchored to a source hierarchy instead of internet folklore.
When there is tension between a popular saying and the primary corpus, prefer the primary corpus.

## 1. Primary Corpus

Use these as the highest-confidence basis for advice:

- `《曾国藩家书》`
  Best for his self-cultivation, family education, reading habits, money sense, and how he corrected people without fully breaking relations.
- `《曾国藩日记》`
  Best for repeated self-inspection, discipline, emotional management, and the move from浮气 to steady routine.
- `奏折、批牍、书信、诗文、读书札记`
  Best for public decision-making, staffing, campaign pacing, logistics, and reputation management.
- `《曾国藩全集》`
  Use as the main umbrella collection when you need the broader corpus in one place.

## 2. Life Stages To Keep In Mind

Read his method through the stage of life it came from:

- `1811-1838`
  Hunan upbringing,科举训练,入京 before major public power. Use this stage for读书、立志、改气质.
- `1838-1852`
  Hanlin and capital official years. Use this stage for学问、交游、做人分寸、日课.
- `1853-1864`
  Building and leading湘军 during repeated setbacks. Use this stage for带队、治乱、用人、求稳、长期战.
- `1865-1872`
  Senior official years under higher political complexity. Use this stage for取舍、收权、声名、残局处理.

## 3. Related Works

Use these as interpretation, not as the final court of appeal:

- `唐浩明` related整理本与研究文字
  Helpful for corpus access, chronology, and context. Distinguish scholarly整理 from his historical fiction.
- `张宏杰` related studies such as传记与分析
  Helpful for modern explanatory framing and personality analysis.
- Other modern研究、家书导读、年谱、学术论文
  Useful for context and cross-checking, especially when clarifying chronology or public reception.

## 4. Popular Attributions And Caution

Treat these as low-confidence auxiliary material:

- `《冰鉴》`
  Often used in popular management culture. Do not rely on it for face-reading or deterministic judgments about character.
- `《挺经》`
  Often circulated as "曾国藩权谋总结". Treat it as later整理性材料 unless independently supported by the primary corpus.
- Viral aphorisms such as `结硬寨，打呆仗`
  These may still be useful as shorthand for his method, but present them as later summaries unless you have a direct source in hand.

## 5. Practical Reading Rule

When generating advice:

1. Start from the primary corpus.
2. Use related works only to interpret or organize the material.
3. If a claim mainly comes from popular business-writing around曾国藩, lower confidence and say so.
4. Never import physiognomy, manipulation, or authoritarian cruelty as if they were the core of his method.
